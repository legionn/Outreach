5. Multiple Forms

So you know how to make a Shape, then fill in a Shape to be a Form,
and you also how how to transform a Form. How do you make multiple Forms?

We do that with grouping.

A group allows a list of forms to be drawn to the screen.
We can make a group by typing: 

group [Form, Form]
replacing every 
Form
with a valid Form.

For example: 
group [filled red (circle 20), filled orange (square 15)]
will draw two forms.

Note: Each form is seperated by a comma
