2. Drawing our Shapes

What you we're supposed to
make didn't look anything like a circle, right?

If you want to be able to see your shape, you have to fill
it with a color.

You can fill a Shape with a Color by typing: 

filled Color (Shape)

replacing:

Shape
with a valid Shape, and
Color
with a valid Color

For example:

These are examples of shapes that have been filled with a color 
filled red (square 10)
filled orange (circle 15)
