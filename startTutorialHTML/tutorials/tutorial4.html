3. Transformations

That looked more like a filled circle!

We call a Shape that has been filled with a Color a Form
Transformations can be applied to forms.

We can transform a Form by typing: 

scale (Number) (Form)
rotate (Number) (Form) 
move (Number, Number) (Form)
replacing 
Number
with a valid Number, and
Form
with a valid Form

The following examples are all valid Forms: 
scale (2) (filled orange (square 15))
rotate (3) (filled blue (rect 20 10)) 
move (10, 10) (filled red (circle 20))
